How To Play Wordle

Goal

  • Try to solve the puzzle before all turns are used

Playing

  • Pick letters that you think will be in the word
  • There will be a blank square for each letter in the word
  • Letters will have various colors: green means it is the right letter in the right place, yellow means it is the right letter in the wrong place, and grey means that the letter is not used in the word
  • The game ends when all guesses (rows) are used up

Good luck!
