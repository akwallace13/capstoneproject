How To Play Hangman

Goal

  • Try to solve the puzzle before the man is hung (10 turns)

Playing

  • Pick letters that you think will be in the word
  • There will be a blank line for each letter in the word
  • Each wrong letter will add a body part to the hangman - solve the puzzle before he dies!
  • If the chosen letter is in the word, it will appear in the word. Otherwise, the hangman gains a body part and the letter is no longer available to guess with

Good luck!
